Cholera is an acute diarrhoeal disease that can kill within hours if left untreated. It caused by ingestion of food or water that contamined by bacterium Vibrio cholerae.

There are between 21,000 to 143,000 deaths due to cholera every year. And up to 80% of cases can be successfully treated with oral rehydration solution (ORS).
